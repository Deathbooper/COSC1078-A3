Ryan

For assignment three I feel as if our team overall worked well. With MS Teams as our only means of communication we still managed to co-operate in an efficient manner. Towards the beginning of the assignment, I was told that I needed to pull more weight in terms of the workload and communication within our team. I managed to complete all my tasks and attend every meeting for this assignment, so I feel as if I did accomplish what I set out to do. The truth is that I was surprised with the high-quality work we produced when we all put our heads together and completed our tasks. This was achieved due to everyone being available and managed properly, which was a big difference from this assignment and assignment two. Even though we did still experience some difficulties with certain group members, we coped with our issues and overcame them by communicating and delegating tasks even further. We always ensured that everyone was on track to meet the soft deadlines, which was an essential aspect of our success within this assignment. However, I do feel that some aspects of our work in this assignment could have been improved, such as delegating tasks based on the amount of work required instead of by the number of tasks. This made certain parts of the workload imbalanced in my opinion. Something I learned about groups with this assignment is that in order to have a successfully functioning group, every member is required to be responsible and accountable for their role and work within the group alongside healthy communication of any information or issues.
